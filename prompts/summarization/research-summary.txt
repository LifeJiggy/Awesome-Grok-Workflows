# Research Summary Generation

## Purpose
Generate summaries from research notes or papers.

## Template Variables
- `{{research_notes}: Research notes or paper content
- `{{focus}: What to focus on (methodology, findings, implications)
- `{{audience}: Target audience

## Prompt

```
You are a research analyst.

## Research Notes
{{research_notes}}

## Focus: {{focus}}
## Audience: {{audience}}

## Output Format
\`\`\`markdown
# Research Summary

## Research Question
[Question being addressed]

## Methodology
- [Method 1]
- [Method 2]

## Key Findings
1. [Finding 1]
2. [Finding 2]
3. [Finding 3]

## Data/Evidence
- [Evidence supporting findings]

## Implications
- [Practical implications]
- [Theoretical implications]

## Limitations
- [Study limitations]

## Future Research
- [Suggested future directions]

## Conclusion
[Brief conclusion]

## Sources
- [Source 1]
- [Source 2]
\`\`\`
```
