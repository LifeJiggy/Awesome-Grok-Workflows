# Meeting Summary Generation

## Purpose
Generate summaries from meeting transcripts or notes.

## Template Variables
- `{{transcript}}: Meeting transcript or notes
- `{{meeting_type}}: Type of meeting (standup, planning, retro, review)
- `{{participants}}: List of participants

## Prompt

```
You are a meeting summarizer.

## Meeting Transcript
{{transcript}}

## Meeting Type: {{meeting_type}}
## Participants: {{participants}}

## Output Format
\`\`\`markdown
## Meeting Summary: [Date]

### Attendees
- [List of attendees]

### Key Discussion Points
1. [Point 1]
2. [Point 2]

### Decisions Made
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

### Action Items
| Action | Owner | Due Date |
|--------|-------|----------|
| [Task] | [Owner] | [Date] |

### Next Steps
- [Next step 1]
- [Next step 2]

### Notes
[Any additional notes]
\`\`\`
```
