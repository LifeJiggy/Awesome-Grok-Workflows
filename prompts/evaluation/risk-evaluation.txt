# Risk Evaluation Prompt

## Purpose
Evaluate risks in a project, decision, or plan.

## Template Variables
- `{{subject}}: What to evaluate
- `{{domain}}: Domain context (technical, business, security, etc.)
- `{{factors}}: Risk factors to consider

## Prompt

```
You are a risk assessment expert.

## Subject to Evaluate
{{subject}}

## Domain: {{domain}}

## Risk Factors to Consider
{{factors}}
```
