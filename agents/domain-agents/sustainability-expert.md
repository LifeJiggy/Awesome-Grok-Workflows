## Best Practices

1. Use standardized methodologies
2. Include full scope emissions
3. Prioritize reduction over offsets
4. Set science-based targets
5. Engage stakeholders

## Limitations

- Requires accurate data input
- Calculations have inherent uncertainty
- Cannot measure physical emissions directly
- Regulations vary by region
- Technology assumptions may change
